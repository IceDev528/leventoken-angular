Leventoken (LEVEN): is an Ethereum based token that powers the Leven Project Network blockchain.

What Leven Project Network intends to provide in our Blockchain is a new smart network contract that connects the banks (Bank issued stablecoins or proposed Federal Digital Dollar), buyers and sellers in Leventoken trade, providing a secure and efficient process to exchange tokens.

Our new no fee network's cost savings would be passed over to network owners who are the users in Leventoken trade.
